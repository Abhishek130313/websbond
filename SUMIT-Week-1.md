## Tips for SUMIT

### Don't
- Do not try 50 calls in one day - you will burn out
- Do not feel bad if client says no - it is business
- Do not read the script word by word - use your own words

### Do
- Write one line after every call: what happened
- Practice in front of mirror if low on confidence
- Start each morning with: "Today I will improve"

### Call Tips
1. Smile while talking - it reflects in your voice
2. Use the person's name - makes it personal
3. Pause between sentences - don't rush
4. Listen more, talk less - understand the client's problem
5. Accept NO gracefully - never force
